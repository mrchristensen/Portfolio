About me

I am a third-year computer science student at Brigham Young University. Currently I am pursuing a BS in Computer Science and a BA in Spanish Studies. I work in the Verification and Validation lab, where I focus on abstract interpretation and other formal methods. Additionally, I am part of the BYU Association for Computing Machinery club (ACM).

In addition to my interest in computer science, I also enjoy Spanish and hispanic culture. My interest in Latin America started when I lived in Chile for a few years. I fell in love with the language, people, and culture and became inspired to pursue a second degree in Spanish studies. I also enjoy linguistics, the outdoors, photography, fishing, and soccer.
